Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Candy Lollipop Saga, including local game data, optional purchases, advertising technologies, and your available choices.

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Candy Lollipop Saga ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a fun and safe match-3 puzzle experience.

Candy Lollipop Saga is a colorful mobile puzzle game where players match candies, trigger combos, clear level goals, and progress through sweet puzzle challenges.

Key gameplay highlights:

  • Hundreds of candy puzzle levels
  • Bright visual effects and smooth board feedback
  • Boosters, combos, and level-based challenges
  • Offline-friendly core gameplay
  • Free download with optional in-app items

By downloading or using Candy Lollipop Saga, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do Not Collect Directly

We do not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Name or email address during normal gameplay
  • Phone number or address book data
  • Precise GPS location
  • Photos, camera recordings, or microphone recordings
  • Social media profile information

2.2 Local Data Storage

The game may store certain information locally on your device to support gameplay, including:

  • Completed levels, scores, stars, and achievements
  • Game settings such as sound or music preferences
  • Boosters, lives, coins, extra moves, and other earned rewards

This information is kept on your device and is not stored in our own player account system.

2.3 In-App Purchases

Candy Lollipop Saga is free to play. Optional in-game items may be offered through in-app purchases handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store billing systems.

We do not receive or store your full payment card information. Payment processing is managed by the platform provider under its own terms and privacy practices.

2.4 Third-Party Advertising

We may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may process limited device or usage information for ad delivery, frequency control, and measurement.

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Advertising identifiers such as IDFA or AAID
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Usage data related to ad interactions

We do not directly control the data practices of those third-party providers.

3. How Information Is Used

The limited information described above may be used to:

  • Save or restore game progress and settings on your device
  • Provide gameplay features, rewards, boosters, and level progression
  • Support optional purchases through your platform store
  • Improve app stability, compatibility, and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising Services

If ads are displayed in the game, third-party advertising providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites and apps

4.2 In-App Purchase Providers

Optional in-app purchases are handled by your platform provider.

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

Candy Lollipop Saga is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13, or under the applicable minimum age in a relevant jurisdiction.

Parents and guardians can use device-level controls to manage ad personalization preferences or disable in-app purchases.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce privacy risk within the structure of the game and its platform services.

  • Gameplay data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Payment processing is handled by established platform providers
  • We do not operate a direct user account database for this game

No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed completely secure, but we aim to keep data handling limited and practical.

7. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time
  • You can disable in-app purchases in device settings
  • You can adjust ad personalization controls through your device or platform privacy settings
  • You can remove locally stored gameplay data by uninstalling the app from your device

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the game, legal requirements, or third-party service relationships. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
