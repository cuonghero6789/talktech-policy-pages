How to Play

1

Match 3 or more jewels of the same color in horizontal or vertical lines.

2

Break obstacles like colored boxes, ice, and locks to reveal the star jewel.

3

Move the star jewel to the bottom to complete the level and unlock the next one.

4

Break jewels faster to score higher points and create massive combos.
