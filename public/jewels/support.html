2. Before You Contact Us

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include the following details in your message when possible:

  • Your device model and operating system version
  • The game level or screen where the issue occurred
  • A short description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • A screenshot, if available

3. Common Issues

3.1 Game Progress Missing

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If the app is removed or device data is cleared, saved progress may be lost permanently.

3.2 Ads Not Loading

If ads do not appear, check your internet connection and try reopening the app. Ad availability may also vary by region, device status, or ad inventory.

3.3 Game Freezes or Crashes

Please close the app completely and open it again. If the issue continues, restart your device and make sure you are using the latest version of the game.

3.4 Sound or Music Problems

Check your in-game audio settings and your device volume settings. If your phone is in silent mode, audio behavior may be affected depending on the device.

4. Troubleshooting Steps

Recommended basic troubleshooting:

  • Close and reopen the game
  • Restart your device
  • Update the game to the latest available version
  • Check available device storage
  • Ensure your operating system is up to date

5. Feedback and Suggestions
