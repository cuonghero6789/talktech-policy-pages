Support for Candy Dash Runner: Sugar Rush

Find gameplay help, troubleshooting steps, privacy information, and contact details for Candy Dash Runner: Sugar Rush.

How to Play

  • Swipe up: Jump over traps, candy blocks, and gaps.
  • Swipe down: Slide under low obstacles.
  • Swipe left or right: Change lanes to dodge barriers and collect coins.
  • Collect coins: Use coins to unlock characters and rewards.
  • Use power-ups: Grab boosts during a run to push your score higher.
  • Keep running: The longer you survive, the faster and harder the run becomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I improve my high score?

Focus on lane changes early, save power-ups for crowded obstacle sections, and collect coin paths only when they are safe.

Can I play offline?

Yes. The core game can be played offline. Ads and some rewarded ad features may require an internet connection.

Does the game have in-app purchases?

No. Candy Dash Runner: Sugar Rush is supported by ads only and does not include in-app purchases.

What happens if I uninstall the game?

Game progress, high scores, settings, coins, rewards, and unlocked characters are stored locally. Uninstalling the app may delete this local data.

How do I turn sound or music on and off?

Open the in-game settings and use the sound or music controls. Your preference is saved locally on your device.

Is my personal data collected?

We do not collect personal information directly. The game stores gameplay data locally, and ads may be served by third-party advertising providers. Read the Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

  • Force close the app and reopen it.
  • Restart your device if the game becomes unresponsive.
  • Make sure your device has enough free storage.
  • Update to the latest version available on the App Store.
  • Check your internet connection if ads or rewarded ads are not loading.
