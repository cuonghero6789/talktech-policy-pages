Keep the escape running.

Gameplay guidance, troubleshooting, and answers for AI Runner: Freedom Escape.

Escape controls

  • Swipe up: Jump over traps, barriers, and gaps.
  • Swipe down: Slide under low obstacles.
  • Swipe left or right: Change lanes to avoid FBA units.

Survival protocol

  • Collect energy cores, components, and data fragments.
  • Use boosts when pursuit intensity rises.
  • React early as every escape becomes faster.

Frequently asked questions

How do I improve my high score?

Prioritize safe lanes, anticipate obstacle patterns, and save temporary boosts for dense pursuit sections. Every collision slows A-01 and brings the FBA closer.

Can I play offline?

Yes. Core gameplay works offline. Ads and rewarded-ad features may require an internet connection.

Does the game have in-app purchases?

No. AI Runner: Freedom Escape is supported by ads and does not include in-app purchases.

What happens if I uninstall the game?

Progress, high scores, settings, energy cores, and unlocked units are stored locally. Uninstalling the app may delete this data.

Is my personal data collected?

We do not collect personal information directly. Gameplay data stays locally on your device, while advertising providers may process limited device data. See our Privacy Policy.

Troubleshooting

  • Close and reopen the app if the game becomes unresponsive.
  • Restart your device and confirm enough storage is available.
  • Update to the latest version available from your app store.
  • Check your connection if ads or rewarded ads do not load.

Need more help?

Include your device, OS version, app version, and a short issue description.
