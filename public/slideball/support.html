Slide The Ball is an addictive puzzle game where your goal is to create a path for the ball to reach its destination.

  • Swipe to slide: Swipe tiles left, right, up, or down to move them
  • Create a path: Arrange tiles to form a continuous path from start to goal
  • Earn stars: Complete levels with fewer moves to earn more stars
  • Use hints: Watch rewarded videos to earn hints when you're stuck

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get more hints?
You can earn hints by watching rewarded video ads. Simply tap the hint button when you're stuck in a level, and you'll be offered to watch a short video to receive hints.
Q: What happens if I uninstall the game?
Your game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the game, all your progress (levels, stars, hints) will be lost. Make sure to keep the game installed if you want to preserve your progress.
Q: How do I turn off sound/music?
Tap the sound button in the top corner of the game screen to toggle sound effects and music on/off. Your preference will be saved automatically.
Q: Can I play without ads?
Ads help us keep the game free for everyone. However, you can limit ad tracking through your device settings (Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Advertising on iOS, or Settings ‚Üí Google ‚Üí Ads on Android).
Q: Is my personal data collected?
No, we do not collect any personal information. The game only stores your progress locally on your device. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
Q: What devices are supported?
Slide The Ball supports iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 11.0 or later. The game is optimized for all screen sizes including iPhone 11, 12, 13, 14, 15 and iPad Pro.
Q: How many levels are there?
The game currently features multiple level packs with hundreds of challenging puzzles. More levels are added regularly in updates!
Q: The game is not responding. What should I do?
Try these steps:
  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the game
  • Check if your device has enough storage space
  • If the problem persists, contact us (see below)

üéØ Tips & Tricks

  • Plan ahead: Look at the entire board before making your first move
  • Work backwards: Start from the goal and work your way back to the start
  • Use hints wisely: Save hints for really challenging levels
  • Take your time: There's no time limit, so think carefully about each move
  • Earn 3 stars: Try to complete levels with as few moves as possible
